# Consent to Participate in Research

### Study Title
Investment Decision-Making Under Uncertainty

### Purpose
You are invited to participate in a research study about how people make financial investment decisions when presented with different types of information. The study examines how visualizations and contextual information affect decision-making.

### What You Will Do
- Answer a few questions about your risk preferences.
- Complete 2 short practice rounds to become familiar with the task.
- View 32 investment decision trials. Each trial asks you to choose between an index fund shown in blue and a structured note shown in green.
- Answer a brief demographic survey and provide optional feedback.

The study takes approximately 15–20 minutes to complete.

### Risks and Benefits
There are no known risks beyond those encountered in everyday life. Your decisions will determine your bonus with no possibility of losing money. The investments themselves are hypothetical. Your participation will contribute to research on how people interpret financial visualizations and information.

### Compensation
You will receive a base payment of $5 for full participation. Depending on your performance, you will also receive a bonus ranging from $0 to $15.

### Confidentiality
Your responses will be collected anonymously and stored securely. No personally identifying information will be linked to your responses. Data will be used for academic research purposes only.

### Voluntary Participation
Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty by closing your browser window.

### Contact
If you have questions about this study, please contact the research team at datacognitionlab@gmail.com.
